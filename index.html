9. The Raid

The Raid is a pure action flick from Indonesia. You get a very little back story in the beginning to the main character you are gonna be following but once the action starts its pure action and nothing is held back. Reading up on this movie it was awesome learning that these actors who did all the fighting scenes had to learn how to hit someone without it hurting. Making the contact real on screen but not to the point of hurting the other actor. Unlike American films that shoot action in so many cuts. These fight scenes are long and do not have many cuts. So the action is in your face and keeping you at the edge of your seat the whole time.
